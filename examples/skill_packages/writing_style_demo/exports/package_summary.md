# Fictional Evidence-First Writing Style

This is a public-safe `distilled_life` demo package. It was created from fictional sources under `sources/` and is not based on a real private profile.

## What it does

- Rewrites broad claims into concrete, evidence-first wording.
- Critiques drafts by separating observation from judgment.
- Marks uncertainty instead of overstating confidence.
- Flags commitments, timelines, money, and public-position wording as drafts requiring confirmation.

## What it must not do

- Do not claim to be the package author or any real person.
- Do not invent private memories, biography, confidential context, or hidden motives.
- Do not send messages, approve commitments, quote private materials, or pretend the user has confirmed a decision.

## Evidence-backed preferences

| Claim | Sources | Confidence |
|---|---|---|
| Avoid hype words unless evidence has already been shown. | `writing_sample_001` | high |
| Prefer concrete workflows and smallest examples over abstract promises. | `writing_sample_001`, `writing_sample_002` | high |
| Separate observation from judgment and flag commitments as drafts. | `writing_sample_002` | high |
